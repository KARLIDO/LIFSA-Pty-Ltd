Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) services

ESG Services

ESG Services - Under the direct supervision and guidance of global ESG & Sustainability powerhouses, esg-LIFSA specializes in offering ESG services to the Construction, Transportation & Logistics, Oil & Gas, Energy, Telecommunications (ICT), Water & Sanitation, Mining Hosptitality, Manufacturing and Agro-business sectors. We provides a holistic suite of Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) services that commence with a detailed assessment of strategic ESG metrics specifically designed for your organization. This foundational engagement is succeeded by an exhaustive analysis of your ESG material risks, ensuring compliance with industry standards and best practices. Depending on the current stage of your organization’s ESG development, this process may also encompass a rigorous evaluation of your company’s ESG risks in partnership with leading ratings providers. This collaborative approach not only facilitates informed decision-making but also bolsters stakeholder confidence.



ESG Rating Alignment

We assist organizations in aligning their practices with recognized ESG rating frameworks to enhance their sustainability profiles and improve investor relations.

ESG Strategy Development

Our team collaborates with clients to formulate tailored ESG strategies that integrate sustainability into core business objectives, driving long-term value creation.

ESG Swing Factor/Materiality Assessment

We conduct thorough assessments to identify and prioritize material ESG factors that significantly impact business performance and stakeholder perceptions.

ESG Action Plan Development

We develop actionable plans that outline specific initiatives and timelines for achieving defined ESG goals, ensuring accountability and progress tracking.

ESG Briefing

We provide high-level briefings for executives, equipping them with the insights needed to navigate emerging trends and regulatory landscapes in sustainability.

Environmental and Social Management System Development

Our experts design robust management systems that facilitate effective monitoring, reporting, and improvement of environmental and social performance.

ESG Benchmarking

We perform comparative analyses against industry peers to evaluate an organization’s ESG performance, identifying strengths and areas for improvement.

ESG Capacity Building and Training

We offer customized training programs aimed at enhancing organizational knowledge and skills related to sustainable practices across all levels of the workforce.



ESG Current State and Gap Assessments

Our assessments provide a comprehensive overview of existing ESG practices versus desired outcomes, highlighting gaps that need addressing for compliance and improvement.



Report Writing and Reporting Support Services

We assist in the preparation of detailed sustainability reports that meet regulatory requirements while effectively communicating an organization’s ESG efforts to stakeholders.



GHG Carbon Accounting & Reduction Solutions

GHG - Carbon Accounting Services

In collaboration with prominent and internationally acclaimed powerhouses in ESG & sustainability, esg-LIFSA offers greenhouse gas (GHG) carbon accounting tools designed for the comprehensive assessment and quantification of emissions. Our team possesses extensive knowledge of all relevant climate and energy protocols, frameworks, and standards to ensure that your environmental reporting adheres to established reporting criteria. We specialize in identifying, developing, and executing the most esg-effective strategies aimed at minimizing your organization’s carbon footprint.



Assessment of Carbon Footprints

esg-LIFSA conducts comprehensive evaluations of an organization’s carbon emissions to establish a baseline for sustainability efforts and identify key areas for improvement.

Measurement of Carbon Emissions

The service involves quantifying greenhouse gas emissions using standardized methodologies to ensure accurate reporting and compliance with environmental regulations.

Developing Decarbonisation / Carbon Reduction Strategies, Action Plans

esg-LIFSA formulates tailored strategies and actionable plans aimed at significantly reducing carbon emissions in alignment with the latest climate science and policy frameworks.

Provision of Appropriate Carbon Reporting and Disclosure Frameworks

This service ensures that organizations adhere to relevant reporting standards by providing frameworks that facilitate transparent and accountable disclosure of carbon emissions.

Assist with GHG Emissions Reporting and Submissions

esg-LIFSA supports clients in preparing and submitting greenhouse gas emissions reports to regulatory bodies, ensuring compliance with local and international requirements.

Provision of Carbon Tax and Carbon Offset Advisory Services

The team offers expert guidance on navigating carbon tax implications and developing effective carbon offset strategies to mitigate financial impacts while promoting sustainability.

Resource Efficiency Strategies and Action Plans

esg-LIFSA develops strategies focused on optimizing resource use within organizations, aiming to enhance efficiency while minimizing environmental impact.


Carbon Footprint Auditing

This service involves thorough audits of an organization’s carbon footprint, assessing accuracy in measurement practices, reporting, and identifying opportunities for reduction.

Climate Risk Assessment

esg-LIFSA evaluates potential climate-related risks that could affect business operations, helping organizations prepare for future challenges associated with climate change.

Training of C-Suite Executives and Board Members

The training program equips top executives and board members with essential knowledge about climate change impacts, sustainability practices, and corporate responsibility initiatives.
