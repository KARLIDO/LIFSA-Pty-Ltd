Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) services

ESG Services

LIFSA specialize in offering ESG services to Construction, Transportation, Oil & Gas, Energy, Water & Sanitation, Mining and Manufacturing sectors. We provides a holistic suite of Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) services that commence with a detailed assessment of strategic ESG metrics specifically designed for your organization. This foundational engagement is succeeded by an exhaustive analysis of your ESG material risks, ensuring compliance with industry standards and best practices. Depending on the current stage of your organization’s ESG development, this process may also encompass a rigorous evaluation of your company’s ESG risks in partnership with leading ratings providers. This collaborative approach not only facilitates informed decision-making but also bolsters stakeholder confidence.



ESG Rating Alignment

We assist organizations in aligning their practices with recognized ESG rating frameworks to enhance their sustainability profiles and improve investor relations.

ESG Strategy Development

Our team collaborates with clients to formulate tailored ESG strategies that integrate sustainability into core business objectives, driving long-term value creation.

ESG Swing Factor/Materiality Assessment

We conduct thorough assessments to identify and prioritize material ESG factors that significantly impact business performance and stakeholder perceptions.

ESG Action Plan Development

We develop actionable plans that outline specific initiatives and timelines for achieving defined ESG goals, ensuring accountability and progress tracking.

ESG Briefing

We provide high-level briefings for executives, equipping them with the insights needed to navigate emerging trends and regulatory landscapes in sustainability.

Environmental and Social Management System Development

Our experts design robust management systems that facilitate effective monitoring, reporting, and improvement of environmental and social performance.

ESG Benchmarking

We perform comparative analyses against industry peers to evaluate an organization’s ESG performance, identifying strengths and areas for improvement.

ESG Capacity Building and Training

We offer customized training programs aimed at enhancing organizational knowledge and skills related to sustainable practices across all levels of the workforce.



ESG Current State and Gap Assessments

Our assessments provide a comprehensive overview of existing ESG practices versus desired outcomes, highlighting gaps that need addressing for compliance and improvement.



Report Writing and Reporting Support Services

We assist in the preparation of detailed sustainability reports that meet regulatory requirements while effectively communicating an organization’s ESG efforts to stakeholders.
